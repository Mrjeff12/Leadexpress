# Lead Express — Phase 1 Business Model ("The First 500")

**Date:** 2026-03-22
**Status:** Approved by Jeff

---

## Product Definition

**One-liner:** "AI that reads your WhatsApp groups and sends you only the jobs that fit you."

### Two Value Streams

**Stream 1 — Group Scanning (passive)**
- Customer gives WhatsApp group invite links
- Lead Express joins manually (within 24 hours)
- AI scans every message, identifies jobs, matches by trade + county
- Sends to customer via WhatsApp: description + poster details + "Talk to them" button

**Stream 2 — Job Forwarding (active)**
- Contractor sends Rebeca: "I have a job, forward it"
- Rebeca confirms: trade, county, urgency, description
- Rebeca sends to all matching contractors (trade + county)
- Contractor clicks → goes to WhatsApp directly with the poster

### Three User States

| State | Gets lead notifications? | Sees contact details? | Gets forwarded jobs? |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free trial** (7 days) | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Paying** ($79/mo) | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ |
| **Expired / Non-paying** | ✅ max 3/week (teasers) | ❌ "Upgrade to see" | ✅ but no details |

### Groups Lifecycle

- Customer adds group → stays in system forever (even if customer stops paying)
- Always scanned
- Paying customer = gets full details
- Non-paying = gets teaser + "upgrade" (max 3/week)

## Growth Plan

### Phase 1: "The First 500" (Month 1-6)

**Model:** Free + Premium $79/mo
**Target:** 500 paying customers
**MRR Target:** $39,500

**Acquisition Strategy:**
- Week 1-2: Manual DM outreach to active contractors in groups we're already in
- Month 1: 20 paying × $79 = $1,580 MRR
- Viral loop: every paying user forwards ~2 jobs/month → 40 free users → 25% convert
- Month 6: 500 paying, ~1,500 free users, ~3,000+ groups in pool

**Key principle:** Every forwarded job is a $0 CAC acquisition channel.
Non-paying users who receive a forwarded job get a teaser → "Upgrade to see details"

### Phase 2: "The Network" (Month 6-12)

**What changes:**
- VIP level unlocked → "Full Network Access" (leads from entire pool, not just your groups)
- New add-on: Network Pass $39/mo (for non-VIP users who want network access)
- Loyal Day-1 customers → already VIP → get Network Access **free**
- New customers → pay $79 + $39 = $118 OR earn points

**MRR Target:** $150,000 (1,500 paying × avg $100)

### Phase 3: "The Marketplace" (Year 2)

**What changes:**
- Job bidding: poster gets 3-5 contractor offers
- Transaction fee: 5-8% on deals closed through platform
- Premium subscription drops to $49/mo (more accessible)
- Revenue shifts to transaction fees

**MRR Target:** $345,000 (~$4.1M ARR)

### Phase 4: "The Platform" (Year 3+)

- Data insights sold to enterprise (insurance, property mgmt, supply chains)
- API for other platforms
- International expansion
- $10M+ ARR

## Core Principle

**No existing customer ever pays more for what they already have.**
- New features = new prices (add-ons)
- Loyal customers earn features through Network Points
- Only ADD value, never REMOVE it
